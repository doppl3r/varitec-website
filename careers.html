Description Varitec Solutions has been a leader in providing engineered HVAC solutions in the southwest region for over 40 years.  Our headquarters office in Phoenix is searching for an experienced Estimator with commercial HVAC experience.  The primary duties of this position are to provide accurate and timely takeoffs, estimates and quotations for the Sales Team and our customers.

Duties and Responsibilities Serve as a representative of Varitec, displaying courtesy, tact, professionalism and discretion in all interactions with fellow employees and the company’s clientele.

  • Preparing timely and accurate quotations
  • Be able to determine equal products from Varitec’s manufacturers and the competition
  • Communicate to management when pricing is not in line with market price and local product knowledge
  • Communicate well with the manufacturers Varitec represents
  • Prioritize the workload
  • Have a thorough working knowledge of required products
  • Other duties as assigned

Requirements Qualified candidates must possess the following:

  • A minimum of a high school diploma is required, college is preferred.
  • Mechanical Aptitude
  • Previous Sheet Metal HVAC takeoff experience is required.
  • Blueprint reading and specification interpretation.
  • Computer skills and proficiency with manufactures software programs, takeoff software, Outlook, Word and Excel is required.
  • Excellent organization skills, accuracy, dependable work habits and good communication skills

Benefits include - medical, dental, vision, life, 401k with match, holidays and vacation.
