Description This multifaceted position is responsible for a broad variety of general accounting functions. Reporting to the CFO, this is a “hands-on” position, managing processes not people.

We are seeking a bright, proactive and organized person who has a strong work ethic and pays attention to details. This person has good interpersonal skills and enjoys working both alone and with a small accounting department team.

Responsibilities

  • Provide accounting assistance to Controller, as needed.
  • Control, process and reconcile general ledger accounts.
  • Bank Reconciliations
  • Journal Entry preparation
  • Balance Sheet Reconciliation
  • Assist and support month-end close
  • Depreciation Schedule Preparation
  • Assist with Special Projects and Ad-hoc requests
  • When encountered, help resolve data integrity issues

Qualifications include:

  • High School degree is required, college is a plus
  • Construction Industry experience is preferred
  • Five year’s minimum accounting experience.
  • Strong knowledge of Microsoft Excel and accounting software (Great Plains)
  • Ability to manage multiple projects and deadlines

We Offer:

  • A Competitive Salary
  • Medical/ Dental/ Vision/Life, 401K with generous Employer Match, Vacation and paid Holidays
  • Career growth opportunities
