Hospitality

HVAC system operation accounts for some of the highest operating costs in the hospitality industry. Hospitality managers must balance this concern with delivering a high level of dependable comfort for their guests. Varitec has the HVAC system and equipment knowledge to minimize the energy consumption of a facility and assure that space conditions (temperature/humidity), as well as acoustics, are positive influences on the overall experience each guest. From state-of-the-art modular/scalable variable refrigerant volume systems to traditional ”Hi-Rise” 4-pipe stacked fan coil systems and related auxiliary equipment, Varitec delivers fact-based economic data to drive decisions regarding the critical environmental aspects of most any hospitality project.
