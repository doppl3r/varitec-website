Light Commercial Products Sales Representative

Post Date: 8/9/2016

Send resume to

cheryll@varitecsolutions.com

Description This position is responsible for growing Varitec’s warehouse, fan, air distribution and light commercial equipment with the mechanical contractor community.

We are seeking an outside salesperson with technical skills including; takeoff, estimate, quote, submittals and ordering of the equipment. This person has excellent interpersonal skills that are necessary to succeed in all aspects of promotion and selling of the assigned products.

Responsibilities

  • Work closely with assigned accounts during project bidding, award and construction processes, including routine visits to their offices.
  • Develop relationships with key personnel at these accounts.
  • Participate with factory visitors and attend seminars.
  • Maintain a working knowledge of assigned product and manufacturer.
  • Stay up to date and familiar with factory contacts, products, model numbers, descriptions, etc.
  • Take off, pricing, select and preparation of proposals from a set of construction documents, plans & specs.
  • Make a timely response to all customer inquiries.
  • Occasional overnight travel to various locations for factory training.

Qualifications include:

  • Minimum of 2 (two) years’ experience in HVAC sales, service or related construction industry
  • Proficiency with Windows applications including Word, Excel and Power Point.
  • Ability to interpret a variety of instructions in written, oral, diagram, or schedule form.

  • Career growth opportunities
