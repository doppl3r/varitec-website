Field Service Technician

Post Date: 12/14/2016

Send resume to

cheryll@varitecsolutions.com

Description The Field Service Technician will serve as a representative of Varitec. The goal of the Technician is to provide an exceptional “owning experience” for the end user and contractor. Start-up and commission of HVAC equipment including troubleshooting and repair issues with equipment upon arrival from the factory or during the warranty period.

We are seeking an outside salesperson with technical skills including; takeoff, estimate, quote, submittals and ordering of the equipment. This person has excellent interpersonal skills that are necessary to succeed in all aspects of promotion and selling of the assigned products.

Responsibilities

  • Work as a team with the Service Coordinator to fulfill customer needs with courtesy in a timely manner.
  • Perform start up, owner training, or equipment troubleshooting and repair as required by the project.
  • Coordination of Varitec activities or its suppliers as well as with the mechanical, the general & often the owner.
  • Interface with Varitec Aftermarket Department to support their service needs as part of their selling activities.
  • Become familiar with factory personnel, product, model numbers, descriptions, etc.

Qualifications include:

  • High School Diploma or equivalent required.
  • Minimum of two (2) years’ experience in construction industry. HVAC install/service experience is a plus.
  • Refrigeration school certification is greatly preferred.
  • Working knowledge of refrigeration machinery, tools and their application.
  • Excellent customer service/communication skills required.
  • Ability to use Windows applications primarily Word, Excel, and Outlook.
  • Valid driver’s license

We Offer:

  • A Competitive Compensation Plan
  • Medical/ Dental/ Vision/Life, 401K with generous Employer Match, Vacation and paid Holidays
  • Career growth opportunities
